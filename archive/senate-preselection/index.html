CYA Section

Rules

  1. Each nominee for preselection must not:
    1. discuss their nomination, any aspect of the preselection or the election with a non-member, except to confirm their candidacy (unless an exemption is agreed to by the VCC);
    2. expend funds for the purposes of communicating with members in relation to the preselection; or
    3. use party resources or meetings to campaign for or promote their candidacy, except as otherwise allowed by this internal policy.
  2. A member must not do anything on behalf of a nominee for preselection that the nominee may not do.
  3. Party bodies, State Councillors, members who hold public office, persons employed by the Party or as staff of members who hold public office must not canvass for or against votes for any nominee.
